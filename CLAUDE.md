## FAQ Section

**Q: How much does it cost for workers?**
A: Free to sign up. Free background check (company-paid on first hire). Free Baink Personal onboarding. You only pay when you get hired.

**Q: How much does it cost for companies?**
A: Free to post your first project. After that, 10-15% of placement fee. WOTC credits captured = 75-80% of credit value goes to you.

**Q: How does WOTC work?**
A: Work Opportunity Tax Credit gives companies $2,400-$9,600 per eligible hire. TraideMarket screens workers automatically and captures credits on your behalf via FastPortID.

**Q: What if a worker doesn't show up?**
A: Worker ratings are public. Verified projects only. No-shows tank their rating and hurt future placements. We also onboard every worker to Baink Personal for financial stability.

**Q: How fast can I hire a worker?**
A: Post a project, get matched within 24 hours. Most placements start within 48-72 hours.

**Q: Can I use TraideMarket as a staffing firm?**
A: Yes. Partner tier ($299-999/mo) includes white-label dashboard, AI sourcing, WOTC automation, and Baink integration. You keep 80-85% of placement fees + 20-25% of WOTC credits.
